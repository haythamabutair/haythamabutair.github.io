Apartment Location in Palestine

With the increase in population, land has been a major issue in that region. Finding a piece of land is difficult and if found, it is quite expensive to purchase. While land in the West bank is cheaper, it is much more difficult and time consuming to reach the Holy City than those living outside of the wall. To the right is a comparision between apartments located in the west bank and those located in Jerusalem.

West Bank (inside wall)
  • Not allowed to visit Jerusalem
  • Checkpoints located everywhere
  • Difficult to acquire permit to visit Jerusalem
  • 1 - 3 security checkpoints will be passed to reach Jerusalem
  • Checkpoints shut down for holidays and/or political reasons
  • Long delays to cross over a checkpoints
  • Apartments are cheaper than ones in Jerusalem ~ $200,000
Jerusalem (outside wall)
  • Difficult to find land
  • Donum ~ $500,000 - $1,000,000+
  • Apartments sold for approximately $350,000 - $800,000
  • 4X more than apartments in Amman and Ramallah
  • Limited homes/land available
  • Increasing property prices in Jerusalem each year
  • One of the most visited sites by tourists
  • Excellent schools/transportation

  • Travel anywhere with no checkpoints

FAQs

  • Is it in the occupied land?

    No. Al-Nafora is located in Jerusalem but not passed a checkpoint.

  • How far from Al-Aqsa is it?

    Al-Nafora is only 5 miles away from Al-Aqsa. It is roughly a 10 minute drive.

  • Can a non-Palestinian own a complex in Al-Nafora?

    Yes. Owning land/property in Palestine is not restricted to only Palestinians. Anyone can purchase an apartment with ease in the Al-Nafora complex.

  • Is it safe for me to live there?

    As much as the media has portrayed Jerusalem to seem like a war zone, it is the opposite. Many tourist flock to Jerusalem year round to witness the historic and religious landmarks. It is safe for any person to visit

  • Would it be cheaper to own property in the West Bank instead?

    The prices for a complex in Al-Nafora is actually the same price as an apartment would cost in the occupied land. There is not much land left in Jerusalem or it's perimeter so purchasing a used/renovated apartment outside of Al-Nafora would cost you 4X as much.

  • Can I rent it out while i'm not there?

    Of course! Many investors have bought units for long term renting investments. You can quickly find a family willing to rent the apartment that you have purchased.
